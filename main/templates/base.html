Bellingham Tech Events (or BhamTechEvents for short) is a unified calender for you to find fun technology groups and events.

We are an all-volunteer group, and we encourage anyone involved with a group to add it in here, or even better, get a leaderly figure (the person that announces when a meeting is would be best) to create an account, and add the group!
